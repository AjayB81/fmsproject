The clients put the whole sector under pressure to update and digitalize its systems and infrastructure. Like other businesses in the financial services industry, insurance clients require constant access to their services and offers 24/7 from any of their devices, including smartphones.

The insurance industry stores and manages data about the majority of the world’s population. Ensuring safe collaboration with vendors and other third parties in a big data industry like this is crucial to guarantee the safety of client data and business continuity. A comprehensive Vendor Risk Management program can help mitigate risks and facilitate collaboration.

START VRM is adapted to the third-party risk management challenges of the Insurance industry. We have grounded our development on insights from collaboration with different insurance companies. START is committed to empowering insurance businesses to put in place safe and flawless cooperation with third parties.
