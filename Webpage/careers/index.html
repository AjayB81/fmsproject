Work-life balance

Flexible Schedule

Flexible Schedule: Family, travel, time zones and lifestyles; high trust allows team members to work on a schedule that meets the collective need.

Unlimited Vacation

We don’t waste time tracking vacation hours. Our employees take the time they need as they need it and enjoy unlimited paid vacation from day one!

Holidays

NCSE observes 9 holidays per year, plus a day off to celebrate our anniversary. Team members who keep additional holidays just block off the day for observance.

Location Flexibility

Remote

We have adapted our culture to be very remote-friendly and all our positions can be performed 100% remotely. Our employees live in various areas of the country with no expectation for relocation. We keep in touch through Slack and every meeting has a virtual option on Google Meet.

Hybrid

Our headquarters is in Baltimore, MD and we have office space in San Diego, CA. Many of our employees who live around these areas choose to work a hybrid schedule. On the days in which many people are in the office, ISE treats everyone to lunch from a variety of local eateries.

Office

Working from an office provides a structured routine when needed, allows for face-to-face communication and collaboration to facilitate more efficient problem-solving. And the kitchen is always stocked.

Interoffice Travel

Whether our employees are working remotely, on a hybrid schedule, or from the office, everyone enjoys a budget for travel and accommodations to work in the Baltimore or San Diego offices for however long the budget allows. This is a unique perk that not many other companies offer.
