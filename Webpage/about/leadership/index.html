Leadership

The leadership team at NCSE comprises of seasoned professionals who bring a wealth of expertise and experience to the table. With a clear vision for the future and a deep commitment to the company’s values and mission, the team is dedicated to driving the company’s growth and success. Their diverse backgrounds, combined with a passion for innovation, make them well-equipped to navigate the challenges of a fast-paced and constantly evolving industry. Under their guidance, NCSE continues to be a leader in its field and a trusted partner to its clients.

Ajay, Founder, CEO

Since he formed NCSE in 2005, Steve has grown the Baltimore-based company into a cyber-threat mitigation and solutions entity widely recognized for its thought leadership, excellence, integrity, and dedication to its clients.

Ajay's unique approach has defined a proprietary methodology that attracts and utilizes a team of leading scientists, academics, and experts who specialize in the mindset of hackers. "You need to think like an attacker to defend against him. And furthermore, we endeavor to understand the business process surrounding our clients' assets; to develop mitigation strategies that promote business continuity, save costs, and still get them secure as fast as possible. Know your enemy; know yourself."

Ajay has also incubated a powerful research division within NCSE, making it one of the few companies to execute technology security evaluation while simultaneously forwarding the state of the art in new attacks and attack defenses. NCSE’s involvement in the research community helps our team anticipate threats to our clients' assets, and allows us to provide cutting-edge modern mitigation strategies.

Ted , Executive Partner

Ted Harrington is the #1 best selling author of HACKABLE: How to Do Application Security Right, and the Executive Partner at Network and Cyber Security Evaluators (NCSE), the company of ethical hackers famous for hacking cars, medical devices, web applications, and password managers. He’s helped hundreds of companies fix tens of thousands of security vulnerabilities, including Google, Amazon, and Netflix. Ted has been featured in more than 100 media outlets, including The Wall Street Journal, Financial Times, and Forbes. His team founded and organizes IoT Village, an event whose hacking contest is a four-time DEF CON Black Badge winner. podcast.

Sara, Director of People and Organizational Development

Sara Pickering is the Director of Talent Development at Network and Cyber Security Evaluators (NCSE). Self-proclaimed HR Nerd, she works with business leaders at NCSE to ensure that people strategy aligns with the company values and culture. Sara strives to build trust and high levels of employee engagement while supporting teams and leaders to achieve their goals. She is passionate about diversity and inclusion in the cyber security industry.
